On the Subject of Piano Paradox

Piano Paradox flavor text

Upon each solve of a non-ignored module, observe the current stage and the blue-colored key.

Each key must be shifted left or right along the piano to reach a goal note.

The last six keys (if such keys exist) must be shifted so that they play A#, A, F, G, A, A#.

For each of the last six keys, every sixth key before it (if such keys exist) must be shifted the same amount.

When all other modules are solved, play all the required keys in order.
